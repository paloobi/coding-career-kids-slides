Teach Your Computer To Do New Things

Your computer can learn to do things that it doesn’t know how to do yet, just like you. What makes coding a little tricky is that when you're talking to a computer, it doesn't understand you the same way a person would.

How do you make a peanut butter jelly sandwich?

Let's say you wanted ot make a peanut butter jelly sandwich. How would you tell your friend how to make a PBJ sandwich?

What happened here?? Unfortunately, even though computers can do a LOT, they really aren't as smart as you and me. The computer doesn't speak any human languages - it does not know what you meant when you said to put two slices together. And it can only do things it understands.

So if you want to teach the computer how to do things it doesn’t know how to do yet, you need to learn how to speak it’s language. And you need to figure out what to do when it doesn't understand what you tell it to do. That's what coding is all about
